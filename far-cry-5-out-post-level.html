Far Cry 5 Outpost Level Design

Personal project to experience level editor

Design Goals:

  • Teaches the player how to have fun intuitively;
  • Rewards players' effort of exploration;
  • Has an engaging flow and rhythm;
  • Prevents players from getting lost and have a clear perception of space and direction;
  • Gives players meaningful choices (multiple approaches);
  • Creates opportunities for players to write their own narratives and stories;
  • Gives players a narrative context that is integrated with the story to immerse in.

Video Walkthrough

Reflection

  • Effective design requires an effective combination of ideation and iteration.
  • The goal of level design is to create immersive, interactive environments that engage players.
  • Using Far Cry 5 level editor allows for practicing real-world design while maintaining the integrity of core gameplay mechanics.
  • Successful level design must adapt to the unique mechanics of each game, which vary by genre.
