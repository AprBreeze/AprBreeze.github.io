I love to share my passion for game design and learn from different perspectives through video content creation.

Content Focus

My video content explores various aspects of game design:

  • Design Analysis - Breaking down game mechanics and systems
  • Game Reviews - Evaluating games from a design perspective
  • System Breakdowns - Deep dives into specific game mechanics
  • Design Philosophy - Exploring the thinking behind game design decisions

Language and Accessibility

These videos are created in Chinese. My apology if you are looking for an English version. I will try to add English scripts in the future to make the content more accessible to a broader audience.

The goal is to bridge language barriers and share game design insights with both Chinese and English-speaking communities.
