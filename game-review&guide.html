Hard West 2 Walkthrough Guide

A comprehensive walkthrough guide to the game systems, mechanics, and tactics

Project Overview

The Hard West 2 walkthrough provides readers with a comprehensive overview of the game's system and mechanics, offering key insights on how to maximize their use for optimal in-game results. Later sections cover detailed level walkthroughs and tips for completing challenges and earning trophies.

In writing this guide, I aim to leverage my game design and analysis expertise to explain the potential intended uses of the mechanics and level ingredients, helping players fully understand and utilize them.
