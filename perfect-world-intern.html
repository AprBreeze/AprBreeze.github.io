LEVEL DESIGNER INTERN

Perfect World 7th Project Team

Project Overview

During my internship at Perfect World Studio, I contributed to the design of open-world exploration systems, blockout standards, and specialized gameplay challenges for a open-world project. My responsibilities involved translating high-level design goals into functional workflows and level design prototypes, inspired by research on industry benchmarks from games like Elden Ring and Genshin Impact.

Key Tasks and Insights

Open-World Design

  • Crafted exploration loops that integrate combat, puzzles, and resource rewards, ensuring meaningful and rewarding player experiences.
  • Learned the 360-degree approach to open-world design, ensuring players can enter areas from any direction while maintaining a consistent and engaging experience.
  • Focused on creating content-rich environments to maintain open-world exploration engagement.

Blockout Prototyping

  • Helped develop and document blockout construction standards to streamline workflows and align team efforts.
  • Emphasized modularity and scalability during prototyping, enabling rapid iteration and consistent results across levels.

Gameplay Challenge Development

  • Designed engaging gameplay challenges, incorporating player mobility, verticality, and spatial exploration.
  • Collaborated with programmers to ensure challenges were functional both offline and online.

Design Approach and Iterative Process

  • Followed a player-first approach, focusing on how mechanics and layouts enhance player agency and immersion.
  • Iteratively refined designs by integrating feedback from lead designers and cross-functional teams between programmers, artists, and QAs.
  • Used a test-and-learn approach to evaluate and adjust gameplay elements for balance and technical feasibility.

Spatial Design and Player Flow

  • Designed layouts with verticality variance to promote strategic exploration and player creativity.
  • Prioritized player flow by optimizing navigation, pacing, and accessibility in large-scale environments using the concept of positive and negative space.

Technical and Analytical Skills

  • Debugged and tested gameplay mechanics to ensure stability, particularly in online environments.
  • Aligned designs with technical constraints while maintaining creative intent and gameplay goals.
