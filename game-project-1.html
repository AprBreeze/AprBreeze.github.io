Project Overview

During this project, I designed an FPS level incorporating stealth mechanics. The theme of the design competition was Verticality.

In this level, I implemented a multi-layered structure. The player starts from the port, infiltrates the cargo ship to search for the missing agent, explores environmental storytelling, uncovers the secrets hidden on the cargo ship, and ultimately escapes from the top deck.

360 Approach

I adopted the 360 Approach for Open World Mission Design developed by Philippe Bergeron, Level Design Director at Ubisoft Montreal.

This design approach allows players to approach the point of interest from various angles while ensuring a consistent experience for all players.

Feedback from Ubisoft Judges

Pros:

  • 1. The overall layout is well-structured.
  • 2. The mission objective is clear and easy to follow.
  • 3. Effective use of notes and ally NPCs enhances the experience.
  • 4. Key and room placements are well thought out.
  • 5. Ammo distribution is balanced and sufficient.

Cons:

  • 1. Some areas of the level feel claustrophobic due to tight walkways.
  • 2. Certain placement issues resulted in gaps in the level, allowing the AI to detect the player unexpectedly.
  • 3. These gaps also caused AI pathfinding issues, preventing them from reaching the player in some cases.
  • 4. The ending feels rushed, as the ladder leading to the next section is placed just outside the room.

Lessons I learned and will put in future practice:

  • 1. I need to develop better spatial management skills to avoid overly narrow areas.
  • 2. It's also crucial to align modular level elements more accurately to ensure proper AI functionality.
  • 3. While I aimed for players to immediately understand their choices at the end, particularly favoring the rescue option, this led me to focus disproportionately on refining that specific experience. I neglected the other option, which taught me a vital lesson: in future projects, I must consider all player choices and ensure each provides meaningful gameplay.
