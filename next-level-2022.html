Stealth Action Game Level Design - Competition Entry

Project Overview

This was my first entry into the Ubisoft NEXT Level Design Competition in 2022. The challenge was to design a stealth action game level with a strong theme, immersive environments, visual references, and clear presentation.

The level had to combine gameplay elements provided by Ubisoft to create an engaging and enjoyable player experience, while supporting multiple approaches and allowing players to approach objectives from all directions.
