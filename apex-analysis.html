Apex Legends Analysis
Combat Mechanics and Motivation for Active Combat

A deep dive into Apex Legends' combat system and player engagement - 2021

Overview

This analysis breaks down the reasons why and how Apex Legend designers encourage players to actively engage in combat. Created in 2021, this was one of my early attempts at game design analysis, marking the beginning of my journey to become a game designer.

Economy System Analysis

This analysis breaks down the economy and currency system in Apex Legends, examining the role each type of currency plays in shaping the player experience.

Through this breakdown, I found that each currency serves a clear purpose, such as boosting daily active users, enabling cosmetic rewards, or driving monetization. This helped me deconstruct the game's economic design from a more systematic perspective.

Combat Mechanics Analysis

Among many battle royale games, Apex Legends stands out with its fast-paced combat and rules that encourage engagement.

In this diagram, I analyze the design choices in Apex Legends that motivate players to fight, as well as elements that may negatively impact this motivation. The goal is to break down how Apex encourages players to actively participate in combat.

Reflection

This analysis was created in 2021. Looking back, it was a bit too text-heavy and lacked conciseness, but it marked the starting point of my journey toward becoming a game designer. Furthermore, the structural and analytical approach to design is a mindset that has benefited me to this day.
